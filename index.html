01: About Us

Who Are We?

We are a Y3 SVIA team aiming to raise awareness of harmful chemicals and materials in everyday consumer products, and to educate and guide those unaware or unsure on making safer, more sustainable choices.

02: Our Mission

Aims & Objectives

🔍

Identify Greenwashing & Misleading Claims

Increase awareness of the hidden harmful chemicals in everyday products by developing the ability to identify greenwashing, fast fashion and misleading claims.

🛁

Conscious & Responsible Consumers

Encourage students to become more conscious and responsible consumers who understand what goes into the products they use daily.

🧠

Critical Thinking

Encourage critical thinking when evaluating everyday products by questioning labels, ingredients, and marketing tactics.

🌍

Protect “Our Planet”

Promote a sense of responsibility and ownership in protecting “Our Planet” through informed, sustainable choices.

05: The Problem

Hidden in Plain Sight

As we consume more and more products, harmful chemicals have hidden themselves in small font behind long, unpronounceable names in ingredient lists; deliberately obscured from everyday consumers who trust the products they buy.

Think you know what's in your products? The truth may shock you.

84,000+

Over 84,000 chemicals are used in commercial products in the US alone, and fewer than 1% have been tested for safety in humans.

Source: Environmental Working Group (EWG) / US EPA Inventory
